FAQ

How do I install the extension?
Visit the Chrome Web Store page and click Add to Chrome.
Does it work offline?
Yes. Everything runs locally in your browser. No data leaves your device.
What types of QR can I make?
Plain text, URLs, Wi‑Fi (SSID/password), and email mailto: (To/Subject/Body).
How do I export?
Use the Download PNG or Download SVG buttons. You can also choose 256, 512, or 1024 export size.
How can I generate a Wi‑Fi QR code?
Open the extension, go to the Wi‑Fi tab, enter your SSID, password, and security type, then click Generate.
Can I add my own logo to the QR code?
Yes. In the Basic tab, upload your logo in the Logo Overlay field and adjust the size slider.
Where is my history stored?
Locally in Chrome’s storage for the extension. Clear it from the History tab.
Can I use it in Incognito?
Yes—enable “Allow in incognito” on chrome://extensions for the extension.
What permissions are required?
Only activeTab (when you use the “Use Current Tab” button) and storage for history.
Where can I report an issue?
Email at support@secaion.com.
