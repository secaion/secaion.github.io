QR Code Generator — Privacy Policy

We respect your privacy. This page explains what data we collect and how we handle it.

Information We Collect

We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal information or sensitive user data.

  • All QR code generation happens locally in your browser.
  • We never send your QR code content to external servers.

Permissions Used

  • activeTab: Used only when you click the “Use Current Tab” button to get the active tab’s URL for QR code creation. It is applied at the time of use and not stored or transmitted.
  • storage: Used to save your QR code history (up to 50 entries) locally in the extension so you can reuse them. This data never leaves your device.

Third-Party Services

This extension does not use analytics, advertising networks, or any third-party tracking scripts.

Your Consent

By using the extension, you consent to this privacy policy.

Changes to This Policy

If we change our privacy practices, we will update this page. Significant changes will be communicated via the extension’s Support page.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, email us at support@secaion.com.
